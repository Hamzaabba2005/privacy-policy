Privacy Policy for CheckMyPlate

This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use the CheckMyPlate app.

Data Collection

CheckMyPlate does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) or user data. When you enter a vehicle registration number, we use this input to provide vehicle details in real-time, but no data is stored or retained after the request is processed.

Data Usage

The registration number you input is used solely to fetch and display vehicle information, such as make, model, and year. We do not store, share, or reuse this data for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

CheckMyPlate may use third-party services to retrieve vehicle information. These third-party services are responsible for their own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those services for more information.

Security

While CheckMyPlate does not store your data, we implement industry-standard measures to protect the communication between the app and the data services it uses.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the way we handle data, feel free to contact us at: support@checkmyplate.com.
